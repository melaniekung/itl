You can find your tire size in a couple spots on the vehicle. Check your tire or the sticker on your door or door jam.

For example, 225/45 R17. 225 indicates width of the tire. 45 calculates the thickness of sidewall. 17 indicates the diameter of the rim.

Both are great for wet and dry conditions. However, All-Weather tires are also rated for cold and snowy conditions like winter tires.
